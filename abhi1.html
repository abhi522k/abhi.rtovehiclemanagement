The purpose of the establishment of the Motor Vehicles Department

Due to industrial development in the state, there is tremendous pressure on transport services in rural and urban areas. Similarly, two-wheelers, four-wheelers, etc. are fast enough for fast transportation. At the same time, the number of road accidents, increasing number of accidents, air pollution, etc. has also increased.Works related to transport, road vehicles, air pollution, registration of vehicles, inspection, driver's license etc. have been increased greatly. In order to handle these works, the Motor Vehicles Department has been created to implement the rules made under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, tax laws, and passage tax laws etc.

General operation in the motor vehicle section

This section requires primarily following tasks under various laws

1.Registration of vehicles
2.Motor vehicle tax and excise tax collection
3.Issuance of permits (public transit / freight traffic)
4.The learner and the Pucca Driving License
5.Trial of driving
6.Issuance of Renewable Certificate of Transportation Vehicles, Renewal
7.Recognize registered vehicles.
8.Transmission of vehicle, entry of mortgage transaction.
9.Handling the transactions related to transport.
10.Mechanical tests of accidental vehicles.
11.Inspection of vehicles for various offenses on the road.
12.Handling AirProfessional works.
13.Inter-state transport agreement related works.
14.Taxes on business taxes.
